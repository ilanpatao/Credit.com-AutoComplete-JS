Credit.com AutoComplete Example

    This is a demo of an AutoComplete script for pulling data from Credit.com and displaying the results in an auto-complete style result box.

    Begin typing your search term in the input box and you will be able to see and get real-time data from Credit.com containing:

    • Available Credit Cards

    The data returned for searches contains:

    • Credit Card Offers
    • Credit Cards by Issuer
    • Current Rewards
    • APR Rates
    • Reviews
